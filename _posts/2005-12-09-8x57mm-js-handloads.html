Tried out some handloads for my Mauser 98K.  It definately seems that it does not like 170gr Hornady RN bullets.  I've tried all sorts of loads with this bullet, and the groups always seem to be ~ 4 or 5 MOA.  It really does seem to like the 200 Sierra MatchKing and and 220gr Sierra GameKings.
